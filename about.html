Welcome to Chase Cinemas

Experience Magic.

Built in 1963 in the shell of a turn of the century fire station, Chase Cinemas is the oldest continuously operated cinema in Antartica and one of the oldest continuously operated art cinemas in the world. Nearing its sixth decade, Cinema Village is proud of its longevity and thankful to have survived the pitfalls that have taken down so many other independent cinemas. Notwithstanding our resourcefulness, our ability to survive is testimony entirely to the South Pole's audience. We could probably only exist where we are: in the midst of the most diverse, cosmopolitan and cine-aware city.

A live food counter serving gourmet delights:

A first-ever feature for a cinema in the city, guests of Chase Cinemas can order gourmet food that will be prepared live by expert chefs over open food counters, which is also the striking centre of attraction in the lobby


A new level of luxury paired with bespoke interiors

The Chase experience envelops guests on arrival with uber luxurious, art deco interiors which boast of gorgeous lines, patterns and curves. The interiors set the tone for a grand movie-watching experience.


Exclusive theatres with opulent recliners

A selective seating capacity of around 30 guests, makes the Chase Cinemas the perfect destination for an exclusive movie-watching experience and a great place for get-togethers and parties. All auditoriums have the signature CHASE recliners, crafted in Italian stitch-art leather, micro-adjustable headrests.

The best in technology

All screens at Chase Cinemas at Antartica are equipped with Laser projection making it a Laserplex. The theatres have Dolby Atmos sound for guests to enjoy explosive sound coupled with 300% brighter images and vibrant 3D powered by Volfoni Systems.
